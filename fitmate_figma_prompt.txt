================================================================================
FITMATE UI/UX GENERATION PROMPT FOR FIGMA AI / GALILEO AI / UIZARD
================================================================================

Copy the prompt below to paste into Galileo AI, Figma AI, or Uizard to generate your FitMate frontend prototype.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PROMPT START
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Design a modern, premium mobile app UI for "FitMate" - an all-in-one AI fitness ecosystem for Vietnam.
App theme: Sleek dark mode (deep charcoal background #121212) with neon green (#39FF14) and active orange (#FF5722) accent colors, utilizing clean card layouts, glassmorphism, and premium modern typography (Inter/Outfit).

The prototype should contain the following 4 key screens:

1. Dashboard (Home Screen):
- A header showing the user's profile with a friendly welcome message ("Xin chào, Hùng!") and a streak counter (e.g., "7 ngày liên tục 🔥").
- A ring-style progress tracker showing today's calorie status (Calories Ingested vs. TDEE target: "1,800 / 2,400 kcal").
- Quick access buttons for "AI Scan Food", "Today's Workout", and "Chat with AI Companion".
- A "Daily Tasks" checklist with gamified badges.

2. AI Nutrition Vision (Camera & Logging Screen):
- A mock camera viewfinder overlay scanning a bowl of "Phở Bò" (Beef Pho).
- AI recognition badge: "Phở Bò - 85% Match" displaying nutritional details: 550 kcal, 25g Protein, 65g Carbs, 15g Fat.
- A prominent button to "Lưu vào nhật ký ăn uống" (Save to Log) and a slider to adjust portion size.

3. AI Workout Planner (Workout Detail Screen):
- A weekly calendar ribbon (Mon to Sun) showing an adaptive schedule.
- Today's workout card labeled: "AI Adaptive Plan: Upper Body & Cardio (Adjusted for your fatigue level today)".
- A list of exercises with thumbnails, sets, reps, and a floating "Bắt đầu tập" (Start Workout) button.

4. AI Companion Chat & PT Marketplace (Hybrid Social Screen):
- Split layout or tab system:
  - Tab 1: AI Companion Chat: An interactive chat interface with "Buddy" (FitMate's AI mascot), showing friendly motivational nudges (e.g., "Bạn đã hoàn thành 80% mục tiêu hôm nay, cố lên nhé!").
  - Tab 2: PT Marketplace: A clean list of verified local personal trainers (PTs) with professional headshots, ratings (e.g., "HLV Mai Xuân Tú - 4.9⭐ (3 năm kinh nghiệm)"), specialty tags (Calisthenics, Weight loss), and a primary "Đặt lịch hẹn" (Book Now) button.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PROMPT END
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

HƯỚNG DẪN SỬ DỤNG (HOW TO USE):
1. Copy đoạn text từ "Design a modern..." đến hết phần mô tả màn hình số 4.
2. Mở Figma AI (Tính năng "Make a design") hoặc các công cụ sinh UI bằng AI như Galileo AI (usegalileo.ai), Uizard.
3. Paste đoạn prompt vào ô yêu cầu để sinh giao diện.
4. Tinh chỉnh lại các chữ tiếng Việt hoặc kéo thả kết nối prototype trong Figma để có bản demo chạy thử mượt mà nhất.
